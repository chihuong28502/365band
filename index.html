365DABAND

We love music

365DABAND is a Vietnamese R&B, Pop and Hiphop group, consisting of 5 members, founded on September 22, 2010, by Ngo Thanh Van - Director of VAA Agency. In addition, the group is also one of the few groups in Vietnam that has ever won a Contribution Award. The group's official fandom name is Stellaris, each fan in Stellaris is called a Stellar, which means "for the stars". . Right before the debut, this name was mentioned by 365daband with the meaning, that the Stellars will be the stars, the stars are always beside 365 and help Ngo Thanh Van's students shine in the sky. on December 16, 2010 at a small stage in Ho Chi Minh City.
